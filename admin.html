How this works: This editor loads lessons.json, images.json, and symbols.json and lets you change them without touching any code. Your edits live in this browser until you click Export All JSON, which downloads the three updated files. Replace the shipping files with the downloaded ones to make your changes permanent. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Two catalogs: Manual (from the Come Follow Me 2026 manual) and Gospel Art (Church-approved paintings from the Gospel Art Book). The viewer has a toggle for end users to switch between them.

Symbols are shown as inline SVG by default. To swap one for a Church-hosted Gospel Language Symbol image, add an imageUrl field via the Schema tab — the viewer prefers it over the SVG when present.

Full JSON view. You can edit directly and click Apply Changes to validate and load. Use Export All JSON at the top right to download.
